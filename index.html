How to keep up with Market shifts

Business approach should undergo changes at a fundamental level in order to handle market shifts. The latest technological trends that can help handle these market shifts are

  • MobileMobile technology is dominating the market are embedded in our dat to day lives
  • Social Networks social networking service is an online platform that is used by people to build social relations with others.
  • Big dataBig data is used to capture, curate, manage, and process data within a tolerable elapsed time.
  • CloudCloud computing provides shared computer processing resources to computers and other devices on demand.
  • Instrumented productsInstrumentation refers to an ability to monitor the level of performance, to diagnose errors .
  • Intelligent/Connected systemsConnected products are products embedded with processors, sensors, software and connectivity.
